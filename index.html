🏆 Featured Projects & Business Impact

City National Bank (CNB) EPM Reporting Transformation
About the Project
Supported a large-scale enterprise transformation project to migrate City National Bank’s financial reporting from a legacy platform to IBM TM1 / Planning Analytics for Excel (PAfE). Owned end-to-end migration, validation, reconciliation, and delivery of critical monthly management reports used by FP&A and executive stakeholders.
Key Contributions
  • Led migration of 5 complex monthly management reports across multiple business lines, ensuring accurate transition from legacy to TM1.
  • Built a reconciliation framework to compare legacy vs. target system outputs and standardize variance resolution.
  • Partnered with Finance, FP&A, and Technology teams to resolve discrepancies and streamline reporting workflows.
Measurable Outcomes
Delivered 5 enterprise reports generating 1,500+ outputs per cycle, reduced manual reconciliation effort through automation, and improved reporting accuracy and consistency across Finance and business stakeholders. Received both an individual performance award and a quarterly team award for contributions to the successful delivery of the transformation initiative.
BlackLine Reporting Automation for RBC's Report to Shareholders
About the Project
Redesigned and automated the BlackLine reporting process supporting RBC’s Report to Shareholders, Supplementary Package and Pillar 3 reports. Eliminated manual comparison and distribution steps during quarter-end cycles by building an automated workflow for reporting and validation.
Key Contributions
  • Analyzed existing reporting workflow and identified automation opportunities across generation, distribution, and review tracking.
  • Built automated solution using VBA, Excel, and SharePoint to eliminate repetitive manual reporting tasks.
  • Implemented validation controls to improve accuracy and reduce risk during critical reporting cycles.
Measurable Outcomes
Reduced manual effort by 80%, saving ~150–200 hours annually across Finance stakeholders, improving reporting turnaround time and enabling faster quarter-end and year-end close activities.
MCAP Residential Whole Loans Portfolio Variance Analysis
About the Project
Led reconstruction of a broken and undocumented reconciliation process for MCAP’s Residential Whole Loans portfolio. Identified root causes of financial discrepancies and rebuilt end-to-end reconciliation across multiple data sources.
Key Contributions
  • Rebuilt a complex three-way reconciliation process across GL, Salesforce, and cash/bank data.
  • Performed deep financial analysis to identify root causes of historical variances and process breakdowns.
  • Identified COVID-related interest deferral process gaps, developed corrective entries, and presented them to leadership for approval and posting.
Measurable Outcomes
Cleared $1.2MM in reconciliation breaks, rebuilt a critical financial control process, and improved audit readiness through standardized documentation and reconciliation procedures.
Enterprise General Ledger Reconciliation Process Reimagined
About the Project
Led automation and redesign of the GL reconciliation process for Mortgage & Loans portfolios. Replaced manual TLM-based investigation workflows with automated break classification, escalation, and reporting.
Key Contributions
  • Automated reconciliation break classification to distinguish issues requiring manual investigation from those eligible for automated resolution.
  • Built escalation workflow routing breaks to responsible business owners for faster resolution.
  • Improved reporting visibility and reduced reliance on manual investigation in TLM.
Measurable Outcomes
Improved reconciliation efficiency by 45%, reduced daily processing time by ~2 hours, and shortened month-end close cycles by 30%, while significantly improving issue tracking and control effectiveness.
